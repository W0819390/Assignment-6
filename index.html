About Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook, known as Jungkook, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and dancer. He is a member of the popular boy band BTS.

BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013. They have gained international fame for their music and performances.

Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea. He attended Baek Yang Middle School and Seoul School of Performing Arts.

He joined BTS as a trainee at a young age and has since been a key contributor to the group's music, including songs like "Euphoria" and "My Time."

Jungkook and BTS have won numerous awards, including Billboard Music Awards and American Music Awards.

His hobbies include drawing, photography, and playing video games.
